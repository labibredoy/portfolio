WEB SYSTEMS DIGITAL CAMPUS

BAUET Website Development

Comprehensive digital transformation for Bangladesh Army University of Engineering & Technology (BAUET)—two integrated web platforms: the Institute Examination Management System (IEMS) and a modernized official university website serving students, faculty, alumni, and external stakeholders.

> INSTITUTE EXAMINATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM (IEMS)

  • Automated exam scheduling with conflict detection on rooms and faculty availability
  • Dynamic seating arrangement generator to minimize overcrowding
  • Digital result processing, publishing, and audit trails for re-check requests
  • Internal collaboration for invigilation, question papers, and schedule changes
  • Role-based student dashboards: schedules, admit cards, results, secure messaging

> OFFICIAL BAUET WEBSITE

  • Fully responsive design (desktop, tablet, mobile) with SEO optimization
  • Academic program overviews, faculty directory with research profiles
  • Dynamic news, announcements, events, and press releases
  • Student services: forms, routines, notices, handbooks—downloadable and searchable
  • Institutional branding, transparency, and marketing for prospective students
GitHub: BAUET_Website →

> GOALS

Replace manual and semi-digital exam workflows and static web pages with synchronized platforms that reduce administrative burden, improve real-time communication, and position BAUET as a forward-thinking digital-first institution—with modular architecture planned for future LMS, analytics, and mobile app integration.
